# North Star

**Build software that feels like a trusted collaborator for planetary-scale problem solving.**

## Principles
1. **Human agency first.** Users can always see, steer, or stop automated actions.
2. **Explain the why.** Every suggestion carries its provenance, trade-offs, and assumptions.
3. **Low-latency craftsmanship.** Performance optimizations are treated as UX features.
4. **Design for remixing.** Every artifact (data, UI, workflows) is forkable and auditable.
5. **Care for the planet.** Measure and minimize compute, energy, and human attention costs.

## Success Signals
- Communities adopt modules piecemeal and still feel coherence.
- Critical decisions cite the software’s reasoning trail.
- The software teaches its users something new each week.

## Anti-Goals
- Centralized lock-in
- Black-box automation with no accountability
- Growth that sacrifices trust for vanity metrics
