# Experience Manifesto

1. **Feel immediate.** Interactions respond within 100 ms whenever possible.
2. **Show your work.** Hover any suggestion to see *why* it appeared.
3. **Co-edit everything.** Humans and AI type in the same space with color-coded presence.
4. **Celebrate closure.** Every completed loop generates a shareable story, not just a log.
5. **Accessible by default.** Keyboard-first, screen-reader friendly, high-contrast skins.
