# North Star

**Make context a commons.** Anyone in your orbit should be able to understand *why* a decision was made, who was affected, and what comes next—without rummaging through a thousand files.

## Pillars
1. **Relational truth** – Everything is framed as relationships (person ↔ artifact ↔ principle ↔ moment).
2. **Transparent cognition** – AI interventions always cite evidence, assumptions, and confidence.
3. **Agency over automation** – Humans can throttle, redirect, or veto automated actions at any time.
4. **Temporal awareness** – Decisions are positioned in time with retrospectives, not just logs.
5. **Ecological empathy** – System tracks cost (energy, attention, carbon) to nudge sustainable choices.

## Success Signals
- Teams can reconstruct any major decision within minutes, complete with rationale trail.
- Personal rituals (week-in-review, commitment check-ins) are auto-generated but feel hand crafted.
- External collaborators trust Tapestry enough to plug their own data streams into it.

## Anti-Goals
- Becoming a social network
- Obscuring model usage or provenance
- Optimizing for engagement metrics over depth of understanding
