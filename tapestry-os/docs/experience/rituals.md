# Experience Rituals

1. **Daily weave** – 5-minute prompt that asks “What shifted today?” and links the answer to people + artifacts automatically.
2. **Commitment pulse** – Weekly digest showing promises made, their status, and nudges to renegotiate.
3. **Decision reenactment** – Interactive playback where you scrub through a decision’s timeline, reading rationale alongside outcomes.
4. **Reflection duet** – Paired mode where you and Tapestry co-write the story behind a project milestone.
5. **Sunset ceremony** – When an initiative ends, the system bundles learnings, credits contributors, and archives the thread elegantly.
